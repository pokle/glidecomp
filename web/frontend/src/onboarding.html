Let's set up your pilot profile

As it appears on competition registrations

3-20 characters, letters, numbers, and hyphens. Used in your profile URL.

Your CIVL ID, email address, name, and other sporting body IDs are used to match you to pre-existing competition registrations. When a comp admin pre-registers you from a spreadsheet, filling in any of these fields here lets GlideComp link that registration to your account automatically — in priority order: CIVL → other IDs → email → exact name.

You can add other IDs (USHPA, BHPA, DHV, FFVL, FAI) and details like phone and glider later on your profile page.
